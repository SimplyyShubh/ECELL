Techno-Care

What is This EXACTLY

E-cell MUJ brings you the opportunity to showcase your entrepreneurship skills by organizing a national level hackathon!

Health problem is an issue that is rising at an alarming rate in India as well as the world. The health care ranking of India is 145 and this is very concerning. To overcome this problem, participants will have to be brainstorming in regard to health care , medical facilities, technology related to the medical field, projects that can control factors causing health problems, etc.

The participants are expected to build a prototype on the problem statement.

The hackathon is open to all the enthusiasts from colleges and schools across India irrespective of their streams. Participants get a chance to grow, expand their exposure and hence earn better outcomes!
